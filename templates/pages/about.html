Working with God

MORTIFIED is a non-denominational, Christian Discipleship and Growth platform that fosters the believer’s journey. We hold fast the tenets of scripture with relation to Christian doctrine and the normal Christian life. We are not bound by geographical factors thereby ensuring that the gospel of the Kingdom reaches out to the believer in every part of the world.

Our Core Values?

Our core values include Christian Accountability, Mentorship and Fellowship.

Our Vision

Our greatest concern at Mortified is the believer’s growth and a walk patterned to that of the Spirit while stressing issues relating to purpose and the advancement of God’s kingdom. Our motivation is the earnest desire to see the growing believer loving God without recourse to the systems of this world and to be actively participatory in God’s agenda for this time and age.

What we do

We undertake trainings for our members who are interested in having help and guidance with their spiritual walk through our various programs targeted at achieving growth in the lives of our trainee members. Refer to our programs page to find an outline of our programs.

Our Team

We have a team of members who are God-fearing, spiritually and morally seasoned, committed and dedicated to the actualization of our vision, and are consistently working to ensure that the work of God is brought to a good finish. They are trained and thoroughly schooled to carry out their functions by God and in good faith.

The Forum

The Forum consists of the entire workforce. It is the bedrock of the Mortified Platform and where decisions pertaining to the network is made. All reports lie to the Forum and must be presented as and at when due. 

The Administrative Department.

This department is in charge of the day to day running of the platform. All questions as to the mode of operation, schedule, the welfare of the trainees and general working of the programme is to be directed to the administration. The department is also in charge of the admittance of members into the platform.

The Pastorate.

This department is made up of the teachers or instructors that provide the daily /lectures that are uploaded on the platform. 

The Academic Department.

This department is concerned with the academic calendar for all our programs. This includes setting out the topics to be taught, books for consideration, bible study and the academic schedule for every programme. They could also on the strength of the reports garnered from the Counsels provide for teachings on topics that directly affect the trainees..

The Training Department.

The training department oversees the training of the workforce. They’re also in charge of Preparation activities for our programs. 

The Follow-Up Department.

This is made up of the Counselors. They are saddled with the responsibility of catering for the spiritual welfare and mentoring of the participants. Their duties include the exacting of a daily check on each trainee and reporting to the Forum per time, to ensure that they are responsive on the platform and to generally cater for their spiritual wellbeing. A counsellor also, is in a position to counsel participants with regards to issues that they may be facing.

The Media Team

The media team consists of tech professionals who are responsible for the management of our online platforms and all media concerns. They create content designed to help the walk of the young believer, and upload them per time on our various media platforms. They are made up of writers, readers, graphics designers, web developers, digital content creators amongst other media related professionals.
